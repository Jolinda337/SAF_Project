  • Key Aspects of Sexual and Reproductive Health:

Sexual and reproductive health refers to a state of physical, emotional, mental, and social well-being related to all aspects of sexuality and reproduction. It encompasses not only the absence of diseases or conditions, but also the ability to have safe, satisfying, and responsible sexual experiences.

Good sexual and reproductive health is crucial for overall well-being and quality of life. Access to accurate information, services, and support contributes to making informed decisions about sexual activity, contraception, and family planning.
